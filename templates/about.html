About Phishing Detector

Educational research project on cyber-security threats.

Our Mission

This website was developed as part of a technical project to address the growing concern of phishing attacks. Our goal is to provide a functional demonstration of how heuristic analysis can be used to identify suspicious digital communications and educate users on prevention techniques.

Key Technologies

The system utilizes an advanced heuristic engine built with modern web technologies. Unlike traditional blacklists, our engine analyzes the structural patterns of URLs and emails in real-time to detect threats like typosquatting, credential harvesting, and social engineering patterns.

Cyber-Security in India

As digital adoption accelerates in India, the frequency of phishing attacks has seen a significant rise. This tool is designed with a focus on localized threats, addressing the unique challenges faced by users in the Indian digital ecosystem, from banking scams to localized UPI frauds.

Privacy & Data Security

User privacy is a core principle of this project. The analysis performed by this tool is stateless—we do not store your scanned links, text content, or uploaded files. All processing is done for immediate results and discarded immediately after the session concludes.
